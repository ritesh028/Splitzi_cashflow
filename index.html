WHO ARE WE

Our aim is to develop a platform based on an algorithm which can restructure cash transactions within groups of people. The algorithm does not aim to change the total amount that any of the participants owes, but it makes it easier to pay people back by minimizing the total number of transactions. Splitzi is a personal finance solution which uses algorithm to minimize the no. of transactions and cash flow in order to settle debts quickly.

WHAT WE DO

We've made a web development project and incorporated our study of Graphs and Data Structures in our website. Our goal is to create a platform based on an algorithm that can reorganise currency transactions between people in groups. The technique does not modify the overall amount owed by any of the participants, but it does make it easier to repay them by reducing the total number of transactions. In technical terms we can say that, given a Directed Graph representing cash transactions, we have to change (if needed) the weights on the existing edges without introducing newer ones.
